An Image of a Largemouth Bass

The largemouth bass is a popular freshwater gamefish. Their aggressive behavior provides excitement and competition for fishermen around the world. The largemouth diet consists of everything from baitfish to smaller mammals. Although preyed upon by a variety of other predators when young, the adult largemouth bass is generally regarded as an apex predator in its environment.

Description of the Largemouth:

  • Green to gray in color,
  • dark to black splotches that form a horizontal stripe,
  • and an upper jaw that extends past the eye; where this bass derives its name.
